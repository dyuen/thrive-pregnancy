BC Housing/Rental Assistance Program (RAP)

  • Although there is a long wait for BC Housing, it doesn’t hurt to be put on the list.

    There are two ways to apply, on-line or through filling out their application form http://www.cra-arc.gc.ca/E/pbg/tf/rc66sch/rc66sch-13e.pdf or call their office at 604-609-7024 to get more information.
    Their address is 101-4555 Kingsway, Burnaby B.C., V5H 4V8
  • Another option for housing is applying for the Rental Assistance Program.

    To be eligible, you need to answer these questions:
    Are you getting rent receipts, are there NO sponsorships in place, have you resided in the province for more than 12 months?
