Youth Clinics

Vancouver Public Health Youth Clinics

Youth clinics offer free and confidential drop in services for youth up to and including age 24. Services are provided by trained health care providers and include:
• Testing for sexually transmitted infections
• Free condoms and water-based lubricant
• Emergency contraception
• Pap tests
• Free or low-cost birth control options
• Pregnancy testing and options counselling
• General counselling
Visit: www.vch.ca/youthclinic for more information and additional youth clinic locations.
