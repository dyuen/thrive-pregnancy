City Reach Food Bank

The Food For Families program is a weekly nutritious food bank for families with children living at or below the poverty line. We offer wholesome, nourishing food appropriate for growing brains.

Tuesday evenings (Doors at 6:00pm and food bank from 7-8:30pm)
Thursday morning (Doors at 9:30am and food bank from 10:30-12 noon)
Our office hours are Monday to Thursday 9am-5pm.

It is run by Broadway Church, but is not in a church building. Located in "the Warehouse" 2650 Slocan St (near Broadway), across from the Van Tech soccer field.  Closest skytrain in Renfrew.

There is a $5 one-time registration fee.  Please bring ID to register.
Contact Damaris or Sarah S for more information.
http://broadwaychurch.com/cityreach/

For Youth Pregnancy and Parenting Program clients:  Good Food Bag

Through the generous donation from ProOrganics, the Good Food Bag program can provide you with fresh fruits and vegetables for only $2.

How to Join:

  • You have to be in YPPP (Youth Pregnancy and Parenting Program) or Healthiest Babies Possible
  • Let your dietitian know you are interested and pay $2. She will sign you up.

    • How to get it:

  • On the Tuesday, pick up your bags at:

    • Evergreen Community Health Centre
    3425 Crowley Dr
    near Joyce skytrain
    (North East Room)

    Tuesday 1:30 – 3:00 pm
    Dates for 2014:
    June 17
    July 15
    Aug 19
    Sept 16
    Oct 21
    Nov 18
    Dec 16

    Please call 604-675-3982 extension 3 if you can't pick up your bag.

  • You can be in the Good food bag program until your baby is 1 year old.
  • If you don't pick up your bag, you lose your $2 and can't sign up again because many people want this program.

    • In partnership with United Way, ProOrganics and Sharing Farm
